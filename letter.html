My Letter to You

Happy 22nd birthday. I’m so proud of the person you are — the way you show up, the way you care, the way you make ordinary days feel like something worth saving.

From 296 nights spent sharing the same pillow to countless adventures large and small, every moment with you has been the best part of my year. This little scrapbook can’t capture it all, but I hope it reminds you just how loved you are.

Here’s to another year of laughter, travel, late‑night talks, and every “let’s do it!” moment we dream up. I love you more than words (or web pages) can say.

— Farah
